Soccer

All my life I've been a big soccer fan. I captained my high school team, and played at the NCAA Division III level at Grinnell. It is still my favorite way to exercise, and I belong to a weekly men's league here in Boston. My favorite clubs are Tottenham Hotspur and Derby County in England, and Udinese in Italy.

Food

I am a lover of good food. I enjoy cooking healthy meals, and have a good repetoire of stews, curries, and marinades. I like trying new foods, but Thai and Indian cuisine are probably my favorites. When I have had the space in previous living arrangements, I have been able to grow a wide variety of vegetables.

Nature

I consistently feel most at-ease when I am surrounded by nature. I enjoy hikes and bike rides, and love a good thunderstorm. I feel lucky to live right by the Muddy River Reservation in Boston, part of the Emerald Necklace network of green space that encircles the city of Boston.

Travel

I have been lucky enough to travel to several different countries and states. I have been to thirty-four states and fifteen countries. If forced to choose, I would probably say New Mexico is my favorite place to visit domestically and the Basque region of France and Spain internationally.
