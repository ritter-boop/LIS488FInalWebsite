About Me

I am a first year Library and Information Science masters student at Simmons University with a concentration in Cultural Heritage Informatics.

Follow this link for my Linkedin

Originally from West Hartford, Connecticut, I graduated with a BS in History and French from Grinnell College in Grinnell, Iowa, in May of 2022. I subsequently spent a year teaching high school
English in rural Gisors, France, for a year. Upon my return, I took some time to decide upon a trajectory for my professional life, but Library and Information Science quickly emerged as a perfect
marriage of my love for primary sources and my desire to improve community.

Besides reading, some of my favorite activities include traveling and exploring new places, both natural and urban; cooking and eating; playing and watching soccer (Come on you Spurs),
and spending time with my cat, Stinkbug.
